FAQ

How long does a typical site take?
Most small-business sites are ready for review within 1-2 weeks after content is received.
Do I need to buy a domain first?
No. We can launch on a free staging subdomain and move to your own domain later if you want one.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes. Starter sites can expand to Growth or Premium by adding pages, forms, or design support.
What kind of maintenance do you offer?
Monthly maintenance includes updates, backups, small content edits, and monitoring.
Will my site work on phones?
All sites are mobile-first and tested for common device sizes.
